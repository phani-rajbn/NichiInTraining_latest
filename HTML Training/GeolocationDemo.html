Using Geolocation Feature

In HTML5, we could use the GPS position of the device that we are viewing the HTML page and get the lat-long cooridinates of the GPS Tracking device. Using that we could Google Maps APIs to fetch the Maps relevent to the location of the user. For this U need internet connection as well as relevant rights to be granted by the owner of the device. GPS positions are best obtained by WIFI, DataConnection or EDGE.
